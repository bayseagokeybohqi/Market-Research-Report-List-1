<p><p>The lipid-regulating drugs market is primarily divided into two categories: statins and non-statins. Statins are widely used to lower cholesterol levels by inhibiting HMG-CoA reductase, effectively reducing cardiovascular risk. Non-statins encompass a range of medications, including fibrates, bile acid sequestrants, and PCSK9 inhibitors, which target different pathways to manage lipid levels. These drugs offer alternative options for patients who may be intolerant to statins or require additional lipid-lowering therapy. The market reflects ongoing innovations and personalized treatment approaches.</p></p>

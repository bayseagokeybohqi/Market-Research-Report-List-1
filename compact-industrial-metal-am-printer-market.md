<p><p>The Compact Industrial Metal Additive Manufacturing (AM) Printer market features three primary technologies: Powder Bed Fusion (PBF), Material Extrusion (ME), and Directed Energy Deposition (DED). PBF utilizes a laser or electron beam to fuse metal powders layer by layer, creating intricate designs. ME involves extruding heated metal filament, allowing for simpler geometries. DED combines focused energy sources to melt and deposit material, ideal for repairs and large-scale components. Each method has distinct applications and advantages in metal manufacturing.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Injectable Blood Thinners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LMWH</li><li>Unfractionated Heparin</li><li>Fibrinolytics</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The injectable blood thinners market includes various types that serve distinct medical purposes. Low Molecular Weight Heparins (LMWH) are commonly used for anticoagulation in patients undergoing surgery or those with deep vein thrombosis. Unfractionated Heparin is typically employed in hospitals for acute treatments and invasive procedures. Fibrinolytics are utilized to dissolve existing blood clots, particularly in emergency settings like heart attacks. Other injectable anticoagulants may include direct thrombin inhibitors and factor Xa inhibitors, catering to specific patient needs and conditions.</p></p>
